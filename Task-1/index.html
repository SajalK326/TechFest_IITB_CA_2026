// 05 — Inquiries

Frequently Asked

Who can participate in TechFest IIT Bombay 2026? +

Anyone. Students, professionals, hobbyists — whether you're from an IIT, a tier-3 college, or self-taught. Some competitions have eligibility caps (e.g. age limit for ICPC-style events). Check individual event pages.

What's included with registration? +

Entry to all events, keynotes, and workshops (subject to venue capacity). On-campus accommodation at IIT Bombay hostels, meals, and a swag kit are included in premium tiers. Basic registration is free.

Can I participate in multiple competitions? +

Yes, as long as their schedules don't collide. Many participants compete in 2–3 events across our 8 verticals. Team overlap rules apply only to certain flagship events.

Will accommodation be provided? +

Yes — IIT Bombay hostels for outstation participants, first-come-first-serve. Off-campus hotel blocks are also reserved at negotiated rates for premium-tier pass holders.

How do I reach the IIT Bombay campus? +

IIT Bombay is in Powai, Mumbai. Nearest station is Vikhroli (Western / Central harbour line). The airport is 20 mins by cab. Detailed travel guide is emailed post-registration.

Are sponsorships still open? +

Limited title and platinum slots remain. Reach out to sponsor@techfest.org with your brand deck for the 2026 prospectus.
